Our Rich History

Dr K. R. Paramahamsa, inspired by his late mother's beliefs on education for all, founded the Paramahamsa Foundation Trust and thereby started the journey of AMC.

"Miracles start to happen when you give as much energy to your dreams as you do to your fears" - Gautama Buddha

1984

Administrative Management College popularly known as AMC was established under Paramahamsa Foundation Trust.

1984

Affiliation with New Port University in California USA.

AMC is one of India's first private institutions to jointly grant courses with a foreign university.

1993

Introduction to postgraduate degree in MBA

AMC became the first private Institution in Karnataka to offer an MBA Postgraduate degree.

1994

Our 52 acre Campus on Bannerghatta Road saw its first batch of students

1999

AMCEC Established with specialisations in Computer Science, Information Science, Electronics & Communication and Telecommunication Engineering

2009

AMCEC gets its first NBA Accreditation

2011

Agreement with KSCA

AMC in partnership with KSCA have developed and managed the cricket ground located on the campus.

2014

Inauguration ceremony

The missile man of India Dr A. P. J. Abdul Kalam pays a visit to AMC.

2016

AMCEC x NAAC Accredited

AMCEC is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).

2017

TCS-iON block

The TCS-iON block was inaugurated by Dr K.R. Paramahamsa on the 15th of September 2017.

2018

AMCEC gets its second NBA Accreditation

2019

Administrative Management gets accredited by NAAC

2021

AMC ranked 2nd in Top Private Engineering Colleges in Karnataka and 3rd in Top Engineering Colleges in Karnataka.

2021

AMC collaborates with Toastmasters International & Harvard Business Publishing

These strategic collaborations with the leaders in the education sector are made with the intent to provide students with world class information, knowledge and case studies.
